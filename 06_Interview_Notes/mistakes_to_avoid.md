# ⚠️ Common Interview Mistakes

Avoiding these mistakes can significantly improve your interview performance.

---

## 🚫 1. Jumping straight to coding

👉 Problem:
Starting to code without fully understanding the question.

✅ Fix:

* Clarify requirements
* Ask questions
* Think before coding

---

## 🧠 2. Ignoring brute force

👉 Problem:
Trying to jump directly to optimal solution.

✅ Fix:

* Start with brute force
* Then optimize step by step

---

## ⏱️ 3. Not analyzing time complexity

👉 Problem:
Writing correct code but inefficient solution.

✅ Fix:

* Always explain time & space complexity
* Compare approaches

---

## 🗣️ 4. Not communicating your approach

👉 Problem:
Silent coding = interviewer has no idea what you're thinking.

✅ Fix:

* Think out loud
* Explain steps clearly

---

## 🔁 5. Not handling edge cases

👉 Problem:
Code works for normal cases but fails on:

* Empty input
* Single element
* Large constraints

---

## 🧩 6. Overcomplicating the solution

👉 Problem:
Trying to use advanced techniques unnecessarily.

✅ Fix:

* Keep it simple
* Use patterns you understand

---

## 😰 7. Panicking under pressure

👉 Problem:
Forgetting known concepts due to stress.

✅ Fix:

* Pause and think
* Break problem into smaller parts

---

## ❌ 8. Giving up too early

👉 Problem:
Stopping when stuck for a few minutes.

✅ Fix:

* Try different approaches
* Ask for hints

---

## 📉 9. Not testing your code

👉 Problem:
Submitting without checking.

✅ Fix:

* Dry run with sample inputs
* Check edge cases

---

## 🧍 10. Weak introduction

👉 Problem:
First impression is unclear or unfocused.

✅ Fix:

* Prepare a structured intro
* Be confident and concise

---

## 💡 Key Insight

👉 Interviews are not about perfection.
They are about **problem-solving approach and clarity of thought**.

---

## 🧾 Final Advice

* Think clearly
* Communicate effectively
* Stay calm and consistent

---

Avoid mistakes → improve performance → get selected 🚀
